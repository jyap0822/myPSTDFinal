JERROS POGI The event will engage participants on strategic imperatives and interventions to accelerate Talent Development. These can help contribute to organizational growth.

Now on its 53rd year, the Philippine Society for Training and Development (PSTD) has grown from a professional organization of learning and development practitioners, experts, consultants, and managers into a real partner and thought leader that influences the talent development ecosystem, its practitioners and business leaders.

The Annual Convention provides a platform for exchanging ideas, new practices and innovative set of tools and solutions on the design, delivery, implementation, and evaluation of L&D programs.

Top three reasons to attend the convention: 

  • Largest conference in the field providing the most choices for your development
  • Top rated learning sessions and expo from renowned industry speakers and luminaries
  • Networking with like-minded peers in the country and other ASEAN member states
